Docker Tip #13 - Use NetBeans 8.2 when editing your Dockerfile

If you are like me you probably have to look at the Docker documentation when you are editing your Dockerfile. As I like to be able to see the documentation while I type I was looking around for an editor that would show the documentation while editing.

And I am very happy to see that NetBeans 8.2 does not only contain Dockerfile syntax highlighting but also popup documentation. I love the NetBeans guys as they keep evolving their product to include the stuff we as developers need.
