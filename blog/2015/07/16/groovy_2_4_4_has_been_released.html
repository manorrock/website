Groovy 2.4.4 has been released

When Groovy had to look for a new home because Pivotal decided to not support it anymore everyone was thinking "What is going to happen to Groovy?"

Well it looks like Groovy is back in its groove. They have just released version 2.4.4 under the Apache umbrella as an incubator project.

Congratz to them!
