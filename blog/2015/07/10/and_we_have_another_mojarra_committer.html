And we have another Mojarra committer

If you have ever wondered how you can get committer access to the Mojarra codebase, it is surprisingly simple. Contribute enhancements, patches and once you have demonstrated that you are good citizen and have signed the OCA you will be granted committer access.

I want to congratulate Arjan from ZEEF on getting committer access. He has been working diligently on both new JSF 2.3 features, but also on bug fixes on the older releases.

So if you would like to follow in his footsteps, start contributing code :)
