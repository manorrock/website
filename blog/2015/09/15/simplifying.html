Simplifying

As I have been contemplating what to best spend my time on I have decided while for large scale projects you need infrastructure for continuous integration, source control, issue tracking, code quality and what not more I do NOT need it all for my pet projects.

So I have decided to ramp down on those so I do not have to maintain them. If one of the pet projects would eventually need it then GitHub, Gitlab, Bitbucket or the like might be a good fit.

While for some folks this might seem a step backwards I say "Less is more!"
